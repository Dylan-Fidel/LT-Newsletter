Industry Retail News

Tokmanni has agreed to sell five retail properties.

This will total to 15,700 square meters and be sold to Cibus Nordic Real Estate AB (publ) for approximately EUR 21.6 million, with closings set for later this year and July 2025.

Carrefour and Coopérative U are launching 'Concordis'.

'Concordis' is a European buying alliance to strengthen their purchasing power through shared volume and international services, starting with the 2026 negotiations. Based in Brussels, 'Concordis' aims to expand by welcoming other retailers while promoting fair supplier relationships and compliance with competition law.

Lidl GB study reveals gaps in womans nutrition knowledge.

As UEFA Women's EURO 2025 2025 kicks off, new research from Lidl GB uncovers a major knowledge gap in female sports nutrition - with one in three women avoiding exercise during their period and 80% never receiving tailored advice.

Wolt and MediaMarkt Partnership

Wolt has expanded its partnership with MediaMarktSaturn to offer rapid delivery in Hungary and Austria, with customers in Hungary ordering from 40 stores via the Wolt app and Austrians using Wolt Drive through MediaMarkt’s webshop.

Pandora and Amazon collaborate to combat counterfeit trade.

Pandora, in partnership with Amazon’s Counterfeit Crimes Unit, successfully dismantled a large-scale counterfeit network operating out of China, leading to the seizure of thousands of fake jewellery items and criminal convictions in Shanghai.

ICA strengthens Swedish presence.

ICA Real Estate Group will acquire Alecta Fastigheter’s 50% stake in Ancore Fastigheter AB for SEK 6 billion (€534 million), gaining full ownership in a deal valued at SEK 8 billion (€711 million).
